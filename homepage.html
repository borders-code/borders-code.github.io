Do You Know Your Rights?

By Founders of Borders
August 9, 2019

The United States has been founded on the premise that everyone within its borders are granted certain rights. A common misconception associated with this idea is those who are not citizens are not permitted the same rights as those who are. However, this is untrue. People come to the United States in hopes of a better life—pursuing the “American dream”— but are met with resistance by the government and its people.
